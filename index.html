Movie Synopsis

Mary Poppins is a film released in 1964 and follows a family in need of a bit of magic. Enters Mary Poppins (Julie Andrews ) whose direct, but loving nature brings the family together by showing them all what is most important: family. This site is for the lover of musicals, and features information about the timeless songs that helped to make Mary Poppins the magical film that we all know and love.
