Emma Langston

Emma is an Olympic medalist, having won 8 medals in aquatics. She found her love for swimming early in life - swimming before she could walk, and hasn't looked back. She swam competitively all through primary school, secondary school, and undergrad. She attended Georgia Tech on a university swimming scholarship, while majoring in electronic engineering. she competed on the state and national levels in the United States, and eventually went on to become a medalist in various Olympic aquatics disiplines.


In addition to her competetive experience, she has coached for 9 years, cultivating several Olympic aquatics medalists. Her programs focus on a healthy diet, building endurance and strength, and improving stroke mechanics. She is passionate and knowledgeable, but her true gift is her supportive and enthusiastic coaching style

Henry Arai

Born in Massachusetts, Henry had the opportunity to play many sports. Ultimately, he ended up with a love of baseball which led him to play in the minor league for three years before moving to the major leagues and playing for four different teams over 10 years.


During his tenure in professional sports, Henry has learned valuable skills from his previous coaches and colleagues, and is eager to pass this on to his clients.


Henry's training programs focus on improving strength, endurance, agility, and mental toughness. He also provides valuable insights on the strategies and tactics of the game that he learned during his time as a player.


Cameron Wells

Cameron is a former Olympic runner who transitioned into coaching after retiring from competition. Throughout his career, he has won 21 Olympic medals. He has a proven track record of developing successful athletes, with many of his clients going on to win medals at national and international competitions.


Cameron is committed to helping his clients achieve their full potential, both on and off the track. His training programs are tailored to the individual needs of each athlete, with a focus on building strength, endurance, and mental resilience.


Cameron's coaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of hard work, goal-setting, and mental preparation. He is committed to helping his clients achieve their full potential, both on and off the track.

Talbot Price

However, he didn't let that setback stop him from continuing his passion for the sport. Instead, he became a coach to help train and develop other young athletes.


Talbot's own experience as a player has given him valuable insights into the physical and mental challenges that young footballers face. Sarah Mac hAidhaearais

Sarah is a retired Olympic gymnast who has transitioned into coaching with the goal of training young gymnasts to compete at the Olympic level. Sarah's experience as a former elite gymnast has given her a unique perspective on the physical and mental demands of the sport, as well as 7 Olympic medals.


She is committed to helping her young athletes develop the strength, flexibility, and focus needed to succeed at the highest levels of competition. Sarah's training programs are tailored to the individual needs of each gymnast, with a focus on building a strong foundation of skills and gradually increasing the difficulty of routines..


Sarah's positive and encouraging coaching style has helped her young athletes develop confidence and a love for the sport. She takes pride in seeing her students achieve their goals, and many of her gymnasts have gone on to compete successfully at the national and international levels. Through her coaching, Sarah continues to inspire young gymnasts to pursue their dreams and achieve excellence in the sport.
