What We Offer

MHR is a state-of-the-art facility designed to provide apex-level training for aspiring Olympians and professional athletes. We feature cutting-edge equipment and technology, including high-performance treadmills, weightlifting equipment, specialized training machines, and full health management.


MHR not only boasts the best and latest equipment, we also have specialised areas for sport-specific training, such as indoor and outdoor track and field areas, an olympic-sized swimming pool, and a fully kitted gymnastics area.


The facility's trainers and coaches are all highly experienced and certified in their respective fields, providing expert guidance and support to athletes at all levels of experience.

Our Pool

Our state-of-the-art training facility features a world-class Olympic-sized swimming pool! Our pool is designed to meet the needs of high-performance athletes, providing the ideal environment for training and competition.

With a length of 50 meters and a width of 25 meters, our pool offers ample space for swimmers to practice and perfect their technique. The pool is equipped with the latest technology, including advanced filtration and temperature control systems, to ensure a pristine swimming experience.

Our pool also features dedicated swimming lanes, starting blocks, and electronic timing systems, providing the perfect setting for athletes to train and compete at the highest level. With professional coaches and trainers available to provide guidance and support, our facility is the ideal location for swimmers to achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

Whether you're an Olympic athlete or an aspiring champion, our Olympic-sized swimming pool is the perfect destination for training, competition, and achieving your dreams. Come and experience the ultimate aquatic training experience today!
