What We Offer

MHR is a state-of-the-art facility designed to provide apex-level training for aspiring Olympians and professional athletes. We feature cutting-edge equipment and technology, including high-performance treadmills, weightlifting equipment, specialized training machines, and full health management.


MHR not only boasts the best and latest equipment, we also have specialised areas for sport-specific training, such as indoor and outdoor track and field areas, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, and a fully kitted gymnastics area.

Our Pool

Our state-of-the-art training facility features a world-class Olympic-sized swimming pool! Our pool is designed to meet the needs of high-performance athletes, providing the ideal environment for training and competition.

With a length of 50 meters and a width of 25 meters, our pool offers ample space for swimmers to practice and perfect their technique. Our pool also features dedicated swimming lanes, starting blocks, and electronic timing systems, providing the perfect setting for athletes to train and compete at the highest level.

Our Gym

high-intensity gym designed for high-performance athletes! Our gym is the perfect destination for athletes seeking to push their limits and achieve their full potential.

Equipped with top-of-the-line strength and cardio equipment, our high-intensity gym provides a challenging and dynamic training environment. Our gym is designed to meet the specific needs of high-performance athletes, with specialized equipment for functional training, plyometrics, and strength and conditioning.

Our Indoor Football Pitch

In addition to our other stellar facilities, we also have an indoor football pitch designed to meet the needs of high-performance athletes. Our pitch provides the perfect environment for football players to train and hone their skills, regardless of the weather outside.

Our pitch is equipped with cutting-edge facilities, including locker rooms and showers, ensuring that players have everything they need to train and compete comfortably.

Wait, There's More

You can further elevate your fitness game at MHR! Our indoor running track provides the perfect environment to train, rain or shine. Recover and relax after your workout in our sauna, and take the plunge in our refreshing ice bath.


At MHR, we offer a comprehensive range of services to help you achieve your health and wellness goals.

  • Mental health services
  • Physical therapy and yoga
  • Dietary planning and supplement management
  • Annual GP checkups
  • Intensive training camps (additional cost)
  • Sponsorship opportunities

We believe that a holistic approach is the key to achieving your goals and living your best life. Our team of experts is here to support you every step of the way. Come check us out today!
